I developed this face detection model based on the VGG16 architecture, utilizing transfer learning for better performance. With approximately 100 self-collected facial images, I augmented the dataset extensively, generating over 3000 diverse samples through techniques like rotation, flipping, scaling, and noise addition. Despite this robust training process, lagging in the video stream could be attributed to factors such as processing intensity, network latency, and resource allocation. Optimizing these aspects can enhance the real-time performance of the application, ensuring smoother video playback and efficient face detection.
